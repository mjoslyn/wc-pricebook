    details: Dealer, operator, wholesale, parts… each tier maps to a WordPress role you already use, with lowest-wins / when-priced / always-override semantics.
  - title: Visibility roles
    details: Hide products or show "Call for Price" for an audience matched by roles and/or specific users, scoped to categories.
  - title: Per-product overrides
    details: Force a product or its price visible to specific roles/users; set per-customer negotiated prices and quantity breaks.
  - title: Manager tooling
    details: An admin-bar switcher to preview any tier or customer, and a /price-flowchart page that explains every resolved price.
  - title: Multi-account aware
    details: Sub-accounts resolve to their parent for pricing and visibility through a single filter.
  - title: Generic & tested
    details: No store-specific IDs in the engine; every knob has a filter; a standalone PHPUnit suite runs with no database.
